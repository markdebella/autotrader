Trade Ideas

These are trade ideas for you to approve or deny — nothing trades automatically. Generate ideas asks the backend for AI suggestions (via Claude), falling back to a deterministic rules engine if Claude is unavailable. When you approve and place one, the backend places the order in paper mode using keys kept in Secret Manager — the browser never holds keys or places orders itself. Each idea shows how it fits within your risk limits.
